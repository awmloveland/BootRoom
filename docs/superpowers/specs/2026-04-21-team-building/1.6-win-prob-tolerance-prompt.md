## What you're fixing

`autoPick` in `lib/autoPick.ts` (around line 168) uses `Math.max(bestDiff × 1.05, bestDiff + 3)` to decide which splits are "close enough to the best" for the tolerance pool. The `1.05` and `3` are magic numbers with no principled grounding.

The fix reframes the threshold as a win-probability band: "accept splits whose score difference corresponds to a win probability within ±`BAND` of 0.5." `winProbability` in `lib/utils.ts` already exposes this mapping. Because it's monotonic in score diff, **the ranking and split selection are effectively unchanged** — this is primarily a rename from magic numbers to a named parameter with a meaningful unit. Don't oversell it as a correctness fix; it's a readability / future-proofing change.
