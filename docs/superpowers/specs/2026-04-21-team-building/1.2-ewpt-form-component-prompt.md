You are implementing a recalibration to the team rating formula in the BootRoom 5-a-side league app. This is the second pass of a two-pass rebalance — the first (April 20) reduced the standout-player and goalkeeper weights; this one removes a form double-count.

**Working directory:** `/Users/willloveland/conductor/workspaces/bootroom/almaty`
**Branch:** `awmloveland/team-ratings-exploration`

## What you're fixing

`ewptScore` in `lib/utils.ts` adds a team-level `avgForm × 0.25` term. But `wprScore` already weights form at 25% per-player, so form ends up counted twice — the effective form weight is ~41% rather than the 25% the formula looks like. This runs counter to the April 20 rebalance goal ("overall quality dominates") and causes a secondary bug: guests and new players (with empty form) drag the team score down.

The fix:

- Remove the team-level `avgForm` term entirely.
- Redistribute the freed 25% weight onto `avgWpr` (making it 0.90).
- Delete the now-orphan `playerFormScore` helper.
