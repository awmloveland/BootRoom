## What you're fixing

`ewptScore` in `lib/utils.ts` (around line 182) awards a `+2` variety bonus when a team has 3+ distinct mentalities. Because `'goalkeeper'` is one of the four mentality values, teams with `{GK + 2 distinct outfielder styles}` qualify — even though the outfield lacks real tactical mix. The keeper is already rewarded by the GK modifier, so counting it toward variety is a double-count.

Fix: filter keepers out before building the mentality set. The bonus now rewards outfielder diversity only.
